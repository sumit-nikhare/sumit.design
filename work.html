I worked on multi-role public health systems used across PHC, SHC, and field settings. The design challenge was translating policy-driven responsibilities into role-appropriate UI, while keeping patterns consistent across modules to reduce relearning.

Across CHIP 2.0, Samasta, and CHO Soft, I treated RBAC as a first-class UI constraint (not just backend logic), standardized navigation and actions, and reduced cognitive load for high-frequency workflows.
